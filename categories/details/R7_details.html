Yamaha YZF-R7

The Yamaha YZF-R7 blends supersport performance with everyday usability. Designed to fill the gap between the R3 and R1, it delivers thrilling handling, aggressive styling, and a torquey twin-cylinder engine – making it ideal for both spirited street riding and track days.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 689 cc
  • Power output: approx. 54 kW (73 hp)
  • Engine type: 2-cylinder, liquid-cooled, CP2 engine
  • Transmission: 6-speed with assist/slipper clutch
  • Fuel system: Electronic fuel injection

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front brake: Dual 298 mm discs with radial-mount calipers
  • Rear brake: 245 mm disc
  • Front suspension: Adjustable USD fork
  • Rear suspension: Monoshock, adjustable preload & rebound
  • ABS: Standard

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 835 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 188 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 13 liters

Key Features

  • R-Series aggressive styling and aerodynamics
  • CP2 engine for smooth, torquey performance
  • Lightweight frame with sporty geometry
  • Assist and slipper clutch for better control
  • Balanced for both street and track use

Price: CHF 10,490.–
