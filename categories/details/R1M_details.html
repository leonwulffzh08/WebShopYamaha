Yamaha YZF-R1M

The Yamaha YZF-R1M is the ultimate expression of Yamaha’s supersport technology. Built for professional riders and serious track enthusiasts, it combines the DNA of the R1 with premium race-spec components. With carbon bodywork, semi-active Öhlins suspension, and the most advanced electronics Yamaha offers, the R1M is a weapon for the racetrack – and a statement on the road.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 998 cc
  • Power output: approx. 147 kW (200 hp)
  • Engine type: 4-cylinder, crossplane crankshaft
  • Electronics: Full ride-by-wire with 6-axis IMU
  • Quickshifter: Up & Down with auto-blipper

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front suspension: Öhlins ERS (Electronically Controlled Suspension)
  • Rear suspension: Öhlins TTX monoshock
  • Front brakes: Dual 320 mm discs with radial calipers
  • Frame: Aluminum Deltabox with magnesium subframe
  • Carbon fiber: Front fairing, tail, and fender

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 860 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 202 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 17 liters

Key Features

  • Carbon fiber fairings for weight reduction
  • Öhlins electronic racing suspension
  • 6-axis IMU with advanced rider aids (SCS, LIF, BC, TCS, EB)
  • Full-color TFT dash with GPS lap timer and data logging
  • Limited production, exclusive finish and materials
