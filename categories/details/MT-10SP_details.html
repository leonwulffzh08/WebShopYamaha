Yamaha MT-10 SP

The Yamaha MT-10 SP is the ultimate Hyper Naked machine. With electronically controlled Öhlins suspension, a premium finish, and MotoGP-derived tech, the MT-10 SP takes everything that makes the MT-10 aggressive and sharp – and pushes it into superbike territory.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 998 cc
  • Power output: approx. 122 kW (165 hp)
  • Engine type: 4-cylinder, crossplane crankshaft (CP4)
  • Transmission: 6-speed with quickshifter & slipper clutch
  • Electronics: 6-axis IMU, slide control, engine brake control, ride modes

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front suspension: Öhlins ERS (Electronically Controlled Suspension)
  • Rear suspension: Öhlins monoshock, semi-active
  • Brakes: Dual 320 mm front discs, 220 mm rear disc
  • Frame: Aluminum Deltabox
  • Cornering ABS & traction control: Standard

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 835 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 214 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 17 liters

Key Features

  • Öhlins semi-active suspension for ultimate control
  • Polished aluminum swingarm and exclusive SP livery
  • 3.5" full-color TFT display with smartphone connectivity
  • Rider aids from the R1 including cruise control & engine brake management
  • Top-tier Hyper Naked performance with track-capable tech

Price: CHF 18,990.–
