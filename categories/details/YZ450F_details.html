Yamaha YZ450F

The Yamaha YZ450F is built for one thing: winning. With its high-powered 450cc engine, industry-leading suspension, and next-level adjustability via smartphone, it’s the motocross weapon of choice for riders who push the limits on the track.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 450 cc
  • Engine type: 4-stroke, liquid-cooled, single-cylinder DOHC
  • Fuel system: Electronic fuel injection
  • Transmission: 5-speed
  • Tuning: Yamaha Power Tuner app with Wi-Fi connectivity

Chassis & Suspension

  • Frame: Lightweight aluminum bilateral beam frame
  • Front suspension: KYB 48 mm USD fork, fully adjustable
  • Rear suspension: KYB monoshock, fully adjustable
  • Front brake: 270 mm disc
  • Rear brake: 240 mm disc

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 965 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 109 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 6.2 liters

Key Features

  • High-output 450cc DOHC engine with linear power delivery
  • Customizable engine mapping via Power Tuner app
  • Revised chassis for improved rider feedback
  • Industry-leading KYB suspension front and rear
  • Factory-level performance straight from the showroom

Price: CHF 11,990.–
