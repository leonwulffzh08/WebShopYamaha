Yamaha YZF-R3

The Yamaha YZF-R3 offers the perfect balance between performance and accessibility. Designed for newer riders and everyday use, it carries the aggressive styling of the R-Series with a lightweight chassis and a smooth twin-cylinder engine – making it ideal for the street and even the occasional track day.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 321 cc
  • Power output: approx. 30.9 kW (42 hp)
  • Engine type: 2-cylinder, liquid-cooled
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Fuel system: Electronic fuel injection

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front brake: 298 mm disc
  • Rear brake: 220 mm disc
  • Front suspension: Upside-down KYB fork
  • Rear suspension: Monoshock
  • ABS: Standard

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 780 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 169 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 14 liters

Key Features

  • Sharp R-Series styling and full fairing
  • Lightweight and beginner-friendly performance
  • Sporty riding position with comfortable ergonomics
  • LED headlights and modern LCD dash
  • Perfect for daily commuting and weekend fun
