Yamaha MT-125

The Yamaha MT-125 brings the Hyper Naked look and attitude to the 125cc class. With aggressive styling, high-end components, and the same torque-rich philosophy as its bigger brothers, the MT-125 is built for young riders who want real street presence and modern performance in a lightweight package.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 125 cc
  • Power output: 11 kW (15 hp)
  • Engine type: 1-cylinder, liquid-cooled, VVA technology
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Fuel system: Electronic fuel injection

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front brake: 292 mm disc
  • Rear brake: 220 mm disc
  • Front suspension: 41 mm Upside-down KYB fork
  • Rear suspension: Monoshock
  • ABS: Standard

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 810 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 142 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 11 liters

Key Features

  • Bold Hyper Naked design with central LED headlight
  • VVA (Variable Valve Actuation) for better power delivery
  • Lightweight frame with agile geometry
  • Modern LCD cockpit and LED lighting
  • A1 license compatible – ideal for young riders

Price: CHF 5,490.–
