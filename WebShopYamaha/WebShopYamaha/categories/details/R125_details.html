Yamaha YZF-R125

The Yamaha YZF-R125 is the entry-level model in Yamaha's R-Series, combining aggressive supersport design with modern technology and premium components. It's the ideal bike for beginners who want style, performance, and quality in the 125cc class.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 125 cc
  • Power output: 11 kW (15 hp)
  • Cooling system: Liquid-cooled
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Starter system: Electric start

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front brake: 292 mm disc
  • Rear brake: 220 mm disc
  • ABS: Standard
  • Front suspension: Upside-down fork
  • Rear suspension: Monoshock

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 825 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 144 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 11.5 liters

Key Features

  • R1-inspired supersport styling
  • Full LED lighting
  • Modern LCD instrument panel
  • Sporty Deltabox frame
  • High-quality fit and finish

Price: CHF 5,990.–
