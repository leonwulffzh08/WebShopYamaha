Yamaha MT-09

The Yamaha MT-09 redefines what it means to ride a naked bike. With its high-torque 889cc CP3 engine, advanced electronics, and ultra-agile chassis, the MT-09 delivers explosive performance and razor-sharp control – all wrapped in aggressive, futuristic styling.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 889 cc
  • Power output: approx. 87.5 kW (119 hp)
  • Engine type: 3-cylinder, liquid-cooled CP3 engine
  • Transmission: 6-speed with assist/slipper clutch and quickshifter
  • Ride modes: 4 modes with lean-sensitive traction control

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front brake: Dual 298 mm discs with radial-mount calipers
  • Rear brake: 245 mm disc
  • Front suspension: Fully adjustable USD fork
  • Rear suspension: Adjustable monoshock
  • IMU & ABS: 6-axis IMU with cornering ABS

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 825 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 189 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 14 liters

Key Features

  • Powerful CP3 engine with refined torque delivery
  • 6-axis IMU with advanced rider aids
  • Aggressive Hyper Naked styling with LED lighting
  • Full-color TFT display and quickshifter as standard
  • Lightweight frame and sporty ergonomics

Price: CHF 10,990.–
