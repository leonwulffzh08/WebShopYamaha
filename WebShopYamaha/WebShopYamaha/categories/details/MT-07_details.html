Yamaha MT-07

The Yamaha MT-07 is one of the most popular bikes in the Hyper Naked segment – known for its raw torque, lightweight design, and all-around fun factor. Powered by Yamaha’s characterful CP2 engine, the MT-07 delivers punchy performance, agile handling, and a unique riding experience that's hard to match.

Specifications

  • Engine displacement: 689 cc
  • Power output: approx. 54 kW (73.4 hp)
  • Engine type: 2-cylinder, liquid-cooled CP2 engine
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Fuel system: Electronic fuel injection

Chassis & Brakes

  • Front brake: Dual 298 mm discs
  • Rear brake: 245 mm disc
  • Front suspension: Telescopic fork
  • Rear suspension: Monoshock, adjustable preload
  • ABS: Standard

Dimensions & Weight

  • Seat height: 805 mm
  • Wet weight: approx. 184 kg
  • Fuel tank capacity: 14 liters

Key Features

  • Torque-rich CP2 engine with linear power delivery
  • Minimalist, aggressive design
  • LED lighting and LCD cockpit
  • Comfortable upright riding position
  • Perfect balance of performance and versatility

Price: CHF 8,990.–
